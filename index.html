MARCH SURVIVOR

Pick a team each day. Win to survive. Last one standing takes all.

How it works: Enter your name and pick one team each day before games tip off. If your team wins, you survive to the next day. You can never reuse a team. Last survivor(s) win the pool.

Add today's games

Enter each matchup. Players will only be able to pick teams from today's games.

Mark results

Click W or L for each team. Marking a winner auto-marks the opponent as a loss and eliminates players accordingly.

Commissioner controls

Advancing the day opens pick submissions for the next day. Reset wipes everything.
